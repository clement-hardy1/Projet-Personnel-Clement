More about C&R PrintLab

What about the startup?

Welcome to C&R PrintLab! Founded by two brothers, Clément and Romain, our startup specializes in designing unique, custom-made objects and figurines using the latest in 3D printing technology. With a shared passion for creativity and innovation, we aim to turn your ideas into tangible, high-quality creations.

Whether you're looking for personalized gifts, custom figurines, or original decor pieces, C&R PrintLab is here to bring your imagination to life with precision and care. Join us as we explore the endless possibilities of 3D design!

The objectives

At C&R PrintLab, our mission is to make custom 3D printing accessible, creative, and meaningful. We focus on three main objectives: - Bring Ideas to Life – We aim to transform each client's unique vision into a physical reality, offering fully customized designs to meet diverse needs and tastes. - Ensure Quality and Precision – Using top-notch 3D printing technology, we prioritize quality, ensuring each piece is crafted with accuracy, durability, and aesthetic appeal.

- Promote Sustainability in Design – By optimizing materials and minimizing waste, we strive to create eco-friendly products that align with a sustainable approach to modern design. Our commitment is to combine creativity with innovation to deliver standout, personalized products that leave a lasting impact.
